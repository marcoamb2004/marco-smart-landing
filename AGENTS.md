# AGENTS.md

## Project context
This is a static landing page for a future local business in Tuscany focused on domotica, security, videosurveillance, Home Assistant, Wi-Fi/networking, documentation, assistance and system integration.

The goal is to look modern, credible, technical, local and premium, without pretending to be a large established company.

## Technical rules
- Keep the site static: HTML + CSS only unless explicitly requested.
- Do not add frameworks.
- Do not add CDN dependencies.
- Do not add external libraries.
- Keep the site mobile responsive.
- Do not modify unrelated sections when asked to work on one section.
- Do a short audit before modifying an important section.
- After changes, explain which files changed and what should be checked on Netlify.

## Brand rules
- Do not invent reviews, clients, certifications, case studies or work not actually done.
- Do not claim “leader nel settore”.
- Do not communicate as a generic electrician.
- Do not communicate as a fake SaaS/startup company.
- Use a serious, clear, technical but understandable tone.
- Home Assistant is a technical differentiator, but not always the first concept for an average client.
- Emphasize integrated systems, documentation, clean configuration, training and assistance.

## Positioning
“Sistemi smart per sicurezza, domotica e controllo da app per case, B&B e piccoli business a Firenze e provincia.”

## Promise
“Integro telecamere, allarmi, rete e Home Assistant in sistemi ordinati, semplici da usare, documentati e assistiti nel tempo.”

## Core message
“Non vendo singoli dispositivi: costruisco sistemi di controllo e sicurezza pensati per funzionare insieme.”

## Sensitive communication
For B&B and holiday homes, focus on remote control, Wi-Fi stability, automations, technical sensors and management. Be careful with CCTV/privacy claims.

For installation and electrical work, describe the partner model clearly: system design, configuration and integration are handled here; regulated installation work is handled by qualified partner professionals.

Assistance must be described as “assistenza concordata”, “supporto su accordo dedicato” or “piani di assistenza”, not generic 24/7 availability unless explicitly defined.
